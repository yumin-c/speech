// Method

Speech audio is encoded with Wav2Vec2-large-960h (frozen) with LoRA adapters (r=16, α=32) on attention projections. Mean-pooled hidden states are concatenated with a 130-dim acoustic feature vector (40 MFCCs × mean/std, F0 stats, RMS, spectral descriptors) and passed through a two-layer classifier.


Training data: Movement Disorders Voice Dataset — Alzheimer (n=55) vs Healthy (n=76) speakers, chunked to 10 s with 5 s hop, 4× augmentation per training file.
